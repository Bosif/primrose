Our mission

My name is Ella Manson, I am from Missouri and I have always felt connected to nature.
I love plants and flowers and getting my hands dirty in the garden.
I started this company 1 year ago in 2022, with the goal of getting other people to love the flora of this beautiful planet.
Did you know that plants are not only beautiful, but they also enrich your health and environment!
I hope to expand my shop in the future, but for now enjoy the limited, but alluring collection of my favorite green friends.
*Two percent of all income goes to the Nature Conservancy to help prevent climate change.*
